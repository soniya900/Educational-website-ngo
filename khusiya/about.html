Khusiya ia an innovative learning organization created to improve the quality of education. It establshed to provide education to childern in the slum area.It is non government organization,Khusiya focused on high quality, low cost and replicable interventions to address gaps in the education system.
To improve the quality of rural education in schools and after school hours by providing access to better educational infrastructure includes digital tools, library, games, financial support and creating progressive and positive learning environment by working with the village community, teachers, government and other stakeholders.
Khusiya is committed to empower the underprivileged section of our society by creating an enabling environment through Encouragement, Education and Employment so that citizens can harness their full potential and live happy and healthy lives with dignity and contribute positively to society.khusiya is working towards shaping up a society.
